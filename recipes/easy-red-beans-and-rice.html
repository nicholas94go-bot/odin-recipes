Easy Red Beans and Rice

Description

This easy red beans and rice recipe is a classic comfort food that's perfect for busy weeknights. It's a one-pot dish that combines tender red beans, savory kielbasa, and fluffy rice in a flavorful tomato-based sauce. The recipe for making this dish involves simmering the beans and sausage together with onions, bell peppers, and garlic, creating a hearty and satisfying meal. It's a great way to enjoy a taste of New Orleans cuisine without the need for a long cooking time. Serve it with a side of cornbread for a complete and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water.
  • 1 cup uncooked Rice.
  • 1(16 ounce) package turkey kielbasa, cut diagonally into 1/4 inch slices.
  • 1 onion chopped.
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped.
  • 1 clove chopped garlic.
  • 2 (15 ounces) cans canned kidney beans, drained.
  • 1 (16 ounce) can whole pealed tomatoes, chopped.
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper.
  • Salt to taste

Procedure of making Easy Red Beans and Rice

  1. Step 1

    Gather the ingredients listed above.

  2. Step 2

    Prepare the Rice: Bring water to a boil in a sourcepan. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water is absorbed, about 20 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    Meanwhile, cook sausage in a large skillet over low heat for 5 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    Stir in onion, green bell pepper, and garlic; saute until tender.

  5. Step 5

    Add in beans and tomatoes with juice. Season with Oregano, pepper, and salt.

  6. Step 6

    Simmer uncovered for 20minutes, serve over prepared rice.
